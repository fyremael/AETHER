# Pitch Deck Outline

This outline is for presentations where the goal is to create serious interest
without overstating the current implementation.

## Slide 1: Title

**AETHER: The semantic coordination fabric for agentic operations**

Goal:
- establish category ambition immediately

## Slide 2: The Operational Problem

Message:
- agentic systems can generate output, but operational truth is fragmented

Show:
- queues, logs, policies, memory stores, and app glue as disconnected islands

## Slide 3: The Cost Of Fragmented Truth

Message:
- teams cannot answer readiness, authority, or incident-replay questions cleanly

Operator questions:
- what is ready
- who may act
- what changed
- why did the system decide this

## Slide 4: The AETHER Thesis

Message:
- the center of gravity should be a semantic kernel, not a pile of integrations

Show:
- journal -> resolver -> compiler/runtime -> query/explain

## Slide 5: What AETHER Combines

Message:
- temporal state + recursive logic + provenance + coordination semantics

Translate into buyer terms:
- memory + intelligence + governed action + proof

## Slide 6: What The Kernel Answers

Message:
- AETHER answers what is true, what follows, who may act, and why

Use:
- `Current`
- `AsOf`
- readiness
- handoff
- fencing

## Slide 7: Flagship Pilot

Message:
- today's proof is not theoretical

Show:
- readiness across dependencies
- lease handoff
- stale-attempt rejection
- durable replay

Evidence:
- Demo 03
- pilot report
- authenticated HTTP pilot

## Slide 8: Why This Matters For Agents

Message:
- LLMs provide fluency; AETHER provides operational truth

Show:
- agents operating inside a governed memory and authority fabric

## Slide 9: Commercial Entry Wedge

Message:
- start where coordination failures are expensive

Examples:
- service operations
- incident response
- compliance-heavy internal workflows

## Slide 10: Product Road

Message:
- narrow pilot now, layered platform later

Show:
- pilot service
- operator plane
- builder plane
- intelligence plane

## Slide 11: Why AETHER Can Become Big

Message:
- this is a candidate control plane category, not a point feature

Phrase:
- "the operational truth layer for autonomous work"

## Slide 12: Call To Action

Options:
- design-partner pilot
- architecture review
- operator demo session
- use-case discovery workshop

## Presenter Notes

- lead with operator pain, not runtime internals
- use technical terms only after interest is established
- keep "current proof" and "platform vision" clearly separated
- always anchor at least one slide in running system evidence
